# About WealthWarriors

## Who We Are

WealthWarriors is an AI-powered financial coaching platform designed to empower individuals on their journey to financial freedom. We combine cutting-edge artificial intelligence with comprehensive financial knowledge to provide personalized, educational financial guidance.

## Our Mission

Our mission is to democratize access to quality financial advice and help people from all backgrounds achieve their financial goals. We believe that everyone deserves access to intelligent financial coaching, regardless of their income level or financial experience.

## The WealthWarriors Team

WealthWarriors was created by a dedicated development team passionate about financial literacy and empowering people to take control of their financial future. Our team combines expertise in:

- Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning
- Financial Planning and Investment Strategy
- Software Engineering and User Experience Design
- Data Science and Analytics

## Our AI Assistant

The WealthWarriors AI Assistant is an intelligent financial coaching system that:

- Provides personalized financial guidance based on your unique profile and goals
- Offers educational information on various financial topics including budgeting, investing, retirement planning, and debt management
- Uses advanced RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) technology to provide accurate, context-aware responses
- Learns from your onboarding information to tailor advice to your specific situation

## What Makes Us Different

1. **Personalized Approach**: We take time to understand your financial goals, risk tolerance, occupation, and income level to provide tailored guidance

2. **Educational Focus**: We focus on empowering you with knowledge rather than just providing answers. Our goal is to help you understand financial concepts so you can make informed decisions

3. **AI-Powered Intelligence**: Our advanced AI technology allows us to process complex financial questions and provide comprehensive, well-researched answers

4. **Accessible and Supportive**: We maintain a friendly, supportive tone and explain concepts in simple terms that anyone can understand

## Our Technology

WealthWarriors is built using state-of-the-art technology:

- **AI Model**: Powered by advanced language models for intelligent conversation
- **RAG System**: Retrieves relevant financial information from our knowledge base to ensure accurate, up-to-date responses
- **User Profiling**: Personalized onboarding system that captures your financial situation and goals
- **Secure Architecture**: FastAPI backend with SQLAlchemy for data security
- **Modern Frontend**: React-based user interface for seamless user experience

## Our Values

- **Empowerment**: We believe in giving people the knowledge and tools to take control of their financial future
- **Integrity**: We provide honest, transparent information and always recommend consulting licensed financial advisors for personalized investment advice
- **Education**: We prioritize teaching financial concepts over just giving answers
- **Accessibility**: We make quality financial guidance accessible to everyone
- **Innovation**: We continuously improve our AI technology to serve you better

## Important Disclaimer

WealthWarriors AI provides educational information and general financial guidance. We are not licensed financial advisors, and our advice should not be considered as personalized investment recommendations. Always consult with a licensed financial advisor before making significant financial decisions.
